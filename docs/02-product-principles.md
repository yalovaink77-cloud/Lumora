# Lumora Product Principles

## 1. Mother-first Experience

Lumora prioritizes the mother’s needs, clarity, dignity, and wellbeing. Decisions should reduce unnecessary burden and respect her role as the primary decision-maker for her own experience.

## 2. Child-centered Data Model

Information related to a child must be treated with care, context, and respect for the child’s evolving needs and rights.

## 3. Family-centered Ecosystem

Lumora recognizes that care is often shared. It should support appropriate family involvement while preserving the mother’s agency and privacy.

## 4. Privacy by Design

Privacy is a foundational product responsibility. Lumora should minimize unnecessary collection, protect sensitive information, and make data use understandable.

## 5. Medical Safety First

Health-related guidance must prioritize safety, appropriate caution, and clear boundaries. Lumora must not create false confidence or discourage professional medical care.

## 6. AI as an Assistant, Not a Doctor

AI may help users understand information and prepare for decisions, but it does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace qualified healthcare professionals.

## 7. Documentation Before Implementation

Product and architectural decisions must be documented, reviewed, and established before implementation. Missing context must be clarified rather than assumed.
